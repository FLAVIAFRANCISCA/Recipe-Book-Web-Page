Roast chicken, the epitome of culinary comfort, beckons with its golden allure and promises of succulent indulgence. With each tender bite, it whispers tales of tradition and home-cooked goodness, filling the air with savory aromas that dance playfully around the kitchen. It's a dish that transcends mere sustenance, elevating mealtime into a celebration of flavor and nostalgia./p>


Ingredients


  • 1 whole chicken , patted dry
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 garlic bulb, minced
  • 1/2 an onion, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1/4 lemon, sliced into 2 wedges
  • 3 rosemary sprigs
  • 100 g unsalted butter , melted
  • 1 cup chicken broth (low sodium) or 250 ml dry white wine
  • 1 tablespoon parsley , finely chopped
  • Variation of spices

How to prepare Roast Chicken:


  1. Preheat oven to 220C/450F (standard) or 200C/430F
  2. Mix together melted unsalted butter, 3 mashed garlic cloves, rosemary, parsley, salt and pepper, and various spices of your choice
  3. Add juice from 1 lemon wedge
  4. Place chicken in a roasting pan. Use a dessert spoon to loosen skin from chicken
  5. Prop chicken upright, drizzle butter under skin, using most of the garlic/herb sludge, but saving a bit of butter for the skin
  6. smear remaining butter all over surface of the chicken. Squeeze over juice of the remaining lemon wedges
  7. Stuff used lemon wedges and rosemary inside chicken. Tie drumstick ends with string and tuck wing tips under the chicken. Sprinkle all over with salt and pepper and spices
  8. Place onion and garlic in the pan, place chicken on top. Pour wine or chicken broth around, drizzle chicken with oil
  9. Transfer to oven. Roast for 10 minutes, then turn oven down to 180C/350F
  10. Roast for a further 1 hr 15 minutes, or until the internal temperature is 75C/165F or until juices run clear when pierced at the join between the drumstick and the body
  11. Baste twice (30 min then at 1 hr), spooning pan juices over skin.
  12. Rest for 15 minutes – don’t cover, skin becomes wet.
  13. Carve and serve with pan juices

How to serve:


  • Any kind of rice you like.
  • Pasta
  • French fries
  • A salad, like greek salad
  • Roast Potatoes
