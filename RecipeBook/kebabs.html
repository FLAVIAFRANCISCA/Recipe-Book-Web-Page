Kebabs

Kebabs a global culinary adventure on a stick, from succulent skewered meats like tender lamb, juicy chicken, and spicy sausage, to vibrant veggie delights featuring grilled peppers, onions, and mushrooms, all seasoned with spices from every corner of the world, making every bite a savory journey.


Ingredients


  • 500g of your choice of meat (chicken, lamb, beef, shrimp, prawns or a combination)
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Variation of spices
  • Skewers (metal or bamboo, if using bamboo soak them in water for at least 30 minutes to prevent burning)

How to prepare Kebabs:


  1. If using chicken or lamb, cut it into bite-sized pieces. If using beef, make sure to trim any excess fat and cut it into cubes
  2. Place the meat in a large bowl.
  3. Add chopped onion, minced garlic, olive oil, your various spice and, salt, and pepper to the bowl with the meat
  4. Mix everything together until the meat is evenly coated with the marinade
  5. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap or a lid and let it marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour, or preferably overnight, to allow the flavors to meld
  6. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat
  7. Thread the marinated meat onto the skewers, leaving a little space between each piece to ensure even cooking (you can add various vegetables in between each pieces like peppers and mushrooms, which should already be prepared)
  8. Once the grill is hot, place the kebabs on the grill grate
  9. Cook the kebabs for about 10-15 minutes, turning them occasionally, until the meat is cooked through and slightly charred on the outside
  10. Once cooked, remove the kebabs from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes
  11. Serve the kebabs hot with your favorite side dishes

How to serve:


  • Any kind of rice you like.
  • Salad
  • Grilled Vegetables
  • Pita bread
  • Roast Potatoes
  • Or you can add a sauce of your choice and have it just like that
